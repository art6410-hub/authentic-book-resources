🎯 ARISE Method

Quick Reference Guide for Emotional Navigation

⚡ The ARISE Method at a Glance

A simple 5-step process to navigate any emotional situation with clarity and wisdom

A

ACKNOWLEDGE

Recognize and name what you're feeling without judgment

R

REFLECT

Pause and examine the source and message of the emotion

I

INVESTIGATE

Explore your options and possible responses

S

SELECT

Choose your response based on your values and goals

E

EXECUTE

Take action and learn from the experience

🔄 Step-by-Step Process

A

ACKNOWLEDGE the Emotion

What to do: "I notice I'm feeling..."
Key phrases: "Right now I'm experiencing..." / "I can feel..."

R

REFLECT on the Source

What to ask: "What triggered this feeling?" / "What is this emotion trying to tell me?"
Key phrases: "This might be because..." / "The message here could be..."

I

INVESTIGATE Your Options

What to explore: "What are my choices here?" / "How could I respond?"
Key phrases: "I could..." / "My options include..." / "Another way would be..."

S

SELECT Your Response

What to consider: "Which option aligns with my values?" / "What serves my growth?"
Key phrases: "I choose to..." / "The best response is..." / "I will..."

E

EXECUTE and Learn

What to do: Take action and observe results / "What did I learn?"
Key phrases: "Next time I'll..." / "This taught me..." / "I learned that..."

🤬 When You're Angry

A: "I'm feeling furious right now"
R: "Someone crossed my boundary"
I: "I could yell, walk away, or address it calmly"
S: "I'll take a breath and speak my truth respectfully"
E: Have the conversation and reflect on the outcome

😰 When You're Anxious

A: "I notice anxiety building in my chest"
R: "I'm worried about the unknown outcome"
I: "I could catastrophize, prepare, or practice acceptance"
S: "I'll prepare what I can and release what I can't control"
E: Take practical steps and practice mindfulness

😢 When You're Sad

A: "I'm feeling deep sadness"
R: "I'm grieving a loss or disappointment"
I: "I could suppress it, express it, or honor it"
S: "I'll allow myself to feel and seek comfort"
E: Cry, talk to someone, or write in a journal

😤 When You're Frustrated

A: "I'm experiencing frustration"
R: "Something isn't going as planned"
I: "I could force it, adapt, or take a break"
S: "I'll step back and try a different approach"
E: Take a break, then return with fresh perspective

📝 Practice Template

Use this template to practice the ARISE method with your current emotional situation:

🅰️ ACKNOWLEDGE: What emotion am I feeling right now?

🆁 REFLECT: What triggered this emotion? What is it trying to tell me?

ℹ️ INVESTIGATE: What are my options for responding?

🆂 SELECT: Which response aligns with my values and serves my growth?

🅴 EXECUTE: What action will I take? How will I learn from this?

⚡ Quick Start Tips

  • Start with just acknowledging the emotion
  • Don't rush through the steps
  • It's okay to cycle back to earlier steps

🎯 For Intense Emotions

  • Take 3 deep breaths before starting
  • Write down each step
  • Seek support if needed

🔄 Make It Habit

  • Practice with small emotions first
  • Use the same trigger phrase: "Let me ARISE"
  • Review your progress weekly

📱 On-the-Go Version

  • Ask: "What am I feeling and why?"
  • Then: "What are my choices?"
  • Finally: "What serves me best?"

🎯 Remember

Emotions are information, not instructions.
The ARISE method helps you receive the message without being controlled by the messenger.
