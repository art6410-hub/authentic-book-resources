🧭 Intrinsic Motivation Compass

Discover your deepest driving forces and what truly energizes your soul

🎯 How to Use This Compass:

• Intrinsic motivation comes from within - it's what drives you naturally

• Answer honestly about what genuinely excites and energizes you

• Focus on activities that feel rewarding in themselves, not for external rewards

• This compass helps you align your life with your authentic driving forces

🔥 Types of Intrinsic Motivation

Understanding the different sources of internal drive helps you identify your personal motivation profile.

🎨 AUTONOMY

"Self-Direction"

  • Desire for control over your work
  • Freedom to choose methods
  • Independence in decision-making
  • Self-directed learning
  • Personal responsibility
  • Flexible scheduling

🎯 MASTERY

"Growth & Excellence"

  • Continuous skill development
  • Overcoming challenges
  • Personal improvement
  • Expertise building
  • Problem-solving
  • Achievement of excellence

💡 PURPOSE

"Meaning & Impact"

  • Making a difference
  • Contributing to something larger
  • Helping others
  • Creating positive change
  • Living values-aligned life
  • Legacy building

🤝 CONNECTION

"Relationships & Belonging"

  • Building relationships
  • Collaborative work
  • Community involvement
  • Emotional connections
  • Team achievements
  • Shared experiences

🎮 CURIOSITY

"Discovery & Learning"

  • Exploring new ideas
  • Research and investigation
  • Creative expression
  • Innovation and experimentation
  • Understanding how things work
  • Intellectual stimulation

⚡ IMPACT

"Results & Influence"

  • Seeing tangible results
  • Influencing outcomes
  • Creating change
  • Recognition of contribution
  • Measurable progress
  • Visible accomplishments
